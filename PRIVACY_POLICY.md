# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 19 July 2026  
**Last updated:** 19 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Mobile PDF Editor** (the “App”) handles information when you use the App. The App is developed and maintained by **NZQueenBee Ltd** (“we”, “us”, or “our”).

## 1. Summary

Mobile PDF Editor is designed as a **local-first PDF utility**. PDF documents, images, signatures, annotations, scans, and edited output files are normally processed on your device. We do not operate an application server that automatically receives or stores the contents of your documents.

## 2. Information the App may access

Depending on the features you choose to use, the App may access:

- PDF files that you select through the Android or iOS system file picker.
- Images that you select through the system photo picker.
- Camera images when you choose to scan a document or add a photo.
- Signatures, drawings, text, dates, initials, stamps, and other annotations that you add to a document.
- Basic app information, such as the installed app version and installer source, when checking whether a Google Play update is available.
- Recently opened file information stored locally on your device to provide the recent-files feature.

The App does not intentionally access files that you have not selected or created through the App.

## 3. How information is used

Information accessed by the App is used only to provide user-requested features, including:

- Opening, displaying, editing, annotating, reorganising, converting, printing, sharing, and saving PDF documents.
- Converting images or camera scans into PDF documents.
- Remembering recently opened documents and theme preferences on the device.
- Checking for and installing updates distributed through Google Play.

## 4. Data storage

Documents and app settings are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to share, export, print, or save them to another service.

Some imported documents may be copied into the App’s private application storage so that they can appear in the recent-files list. Removing an item from the recent-files list may also remove the App-managed copy, but it does not delete the original file held by another provider unless the operating system or that provider performs the deletion.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information.

The App may transfer data only when you initiate an action such as:

- Sharing or exporting a PDF through the operating system share sheet.
- Printing a PDF through the operating system printing service.
- Saving a PDF to a storage provider selected in the system file picker.
- Selecting Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, email, messaging, or another installed service as a destination.

Those third-party services process data under their own privacy policies and terms.

## 6. Google Play services and updates

The App includes support for Google Play in-app updates. When the App was installed through Google Play, it may request update availability information from Google Play and display an update flow. Google Play may process device, account, installation, diagnostic, and usage information according to Google’s policies.

The App itself does not receive your Google account password or payment information.

## 7. Google Drive integration

Direct Google Drive OAuth integration is currently disabled or under development. The App may still access Google Drive through the Android or iOS system file picker when you deliberately select Google Drive as a file source or destination.

If direct Google Drive integration is enabled in a future release, this Privacy Policy will be updated before the feature is made available. The App is intended to request only the minimum Google Drive permissions required for files opened or created through the App.

## 8. Camera and photo permissions

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan a document or capture an image. Photo access is used only when you choose an image to add to or convert into a PDF.

On supported Android versions, the App uses the system photo picker where possible so that broad access to the photo library is not required.

## 9. Security

We use platform-provided storage, file pickers, permission controls, and update mechanisms. However, no software or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device, backups, exported files, passwords, and any third-party accounts you use.

## 10. Data retention and deletion

Because documents are generally processed locally, you control their retention through your device and chosen storage providers.

You can remove App-managed recent files from within the App or delete the App to remove its private local data. Files previously exported to another location must be deleted from that location separately.

The App does not currently provide user accounts, so there is no server-side user account or cloud profile to delete.

## 11. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed specifically to children and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information through an application server. Parents and guardians should supervise the documents and services used by children.

## 12. International users

Files may be transferred to third-party services located in other countries only when you choose such a service for sharing, storage, printing, or backup. The privacy rules of the chosen provider may differ from those in your country.

## 13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when features, permissions, third-party services, or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this document will be updated when material changes are made.

## 14. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, please open a private or public support request through the project’s GitHub repository:

- Repository: `https://github.com/nzfreeman/mobile-pdf-editor`
- Issues: `https://github.com/nzfreeman/mobile-pdf-editor/issues`

Do not include confidential documents, passwords, government identifiers, financial information, medical information, or other sensitive content in a public GitHub issue.
